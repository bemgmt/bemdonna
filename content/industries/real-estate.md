title: Real Estate Operations with AI

summary:
Real estate businesses operate in a high-speed, communication-driven environment. Agents, brokers, and teams must respond quickly to inquiries, coordinate showings, manage follow-ups, and maintain compliance documentation. DONNA functions as a unified operational layer that standardizes communication, scheduling, and lead qualification without adding administrative overhead.

commonWorkflows:
- Lead intake and qualification from portals, website forms, calls, and SMS
- Showing scheduling and confirmation across multiple agents
- Follow-up sequences for new listings and buyer inquiries
- Open house registration and post-event outreach
- Transaction coordination reminders and deadline tracking
- Cross-referral coordination between agents, lenders, and service providers

complianceNotes:
Real estate operations require:
- Clear documentation of communications
- Accurate record keeping for transactions
- Permission-based SMS and email outreach
- Secure storage of client data and contracts

AI workflows should log:
- Timestamped communication records
- Appointment confirmations
- Escalation notes
- Transaction milestone tracking

Sensitive financial or contract language should trigger human review when necessary.

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Map all inbound lead sources (MLS, portals, website, phone).
- Integrate calendar and CRM systems.
- Deploy automated lead acknowledgment and qualification.
- Configure showing scheduling workflows.

60 Day:
- Launch structured follow-up sequences for buyers and sellers.
- Implement cross-channel conversation logging.
- Train agents on escalation and override protocols.

90 Day:
- Analyze response time and conversion metrics.
- Optimize lead routing rules.
- Introduce referral coordination workflows between partner businesses.

KPIsToTrack:
- Average lead response time
- Showing booking rate
- Conversion rate from inquiry to appointment
- Transaction timeline adherence
- Referral conversion percentage
