title: Health and Beauty Operations with AI

summary:
Health and beauty businesses depend on appointment reliability, client retention, and consistent communication. Salons, spas, aesthetic clinics, and wellness providers manage high message volume with limited staff. DONNA streamlines scheduling, reminders, intake, and follow-up while maintaining a professional and compliant communication framework.

commonWorkflows:
- Appointment booking and rescheduling
- Automated reminder sequences
- Intake form coordination
- Service-specific pre-appointment instructions
- Post-appointment follow-up and review requests
- Rebooking and retention campaigns

complianceNotes:
Health and beauty businesses should consider:
- Secure handling of client personal information
- HIPAA alignment for medical aesthetic providers where applicable
- Consent management for SMS and promotional outreach
- Clear documentation of client communication history

AI workflows should:
- Log appointment confirmations and reminders
- Escalate medical or billing inquiries to qualified staff
- Restrict access to sensitive health information
- Maintain opt-in tracking for marketing messages

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate scheduling system with calendar tools.
- Configure automated confirmation and reminder workflows.
- Create structured intake prompts for service types.

60 Day:
- Launch rebooking sequences based on service intervals.
- Automate review requests post-appointment.
- Monitor no-show patterns and adjust reminder cadence.

90 Day:
- Analyze booking-to-show conversion rates.
- Implement loyalty and retention messaging workflows.
- Refine intake qualification to reduce unqualified bookings.

KPIsToTrack:
- No-show rate
- Booking-to-show percentage
- Average rebooking interval
- Client retention rate
- Revenue per appointment
