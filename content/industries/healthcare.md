title: Healthcare Operations with AI

summary:
Healthcare providers manage high volumes of patient communication, appointment coordination, intake documentation, and follow-up care. Speed and accuracy must be balanced with strict privacy and regulatory requirements. DONNA provides a structured operational layer that standardizes communication, reduces administrative burden, and supports compliance-ready workflows.

commonWorkflows:
- New patient intake and qualification
- Appointment scheduling and reminders
- Pre-visit documentation coordination
- Post-visit follow-up and care instructions
- Billing inquiry routing
- Internal task reminders and deadline tracking

complianceNotes:
Healthcare organizations must prioritize:
- HIPAA-aligned data handling
- Encrypted data transmission and storage
- Role-based access to patient information
- Clear documentation of patient communications
- Escalation of clinical or medical advice to licensed professionals

AI systems should:
- Log patient communication history with timestamps
- Restrict access to protected health information
- Trigger human review for medical advice or prescription-related inquiries
- Maintain audit trails for compliance oversight

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate scheduling and EHR-adjacent systems where applicable.
- Deploy structured appointment booking workflows.
- Configure automated confirmations and reminders.
- Establish escalation boundaries for medical decision-making.

60 Day:
- Launch structured intake workflows for new patients.
- Automate follow-up instructions post-appointment.
- Implement centralized communication dashboard.

90 Day:
- Analyze no-show rates and optimize reminder cadence.
- Refine intake qualification for specialty services.
- Review compliance controls and access permissions.

KPIsToTrack:
- Appointment response time
- No-show rate
- Patient intake completion rate
- Average time to respond to patient inquiries
- Administrative workload reduction percentage
