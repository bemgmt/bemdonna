title: Retail Operations with AI

summary:
Retail businesses manage customer inquiries, order updates, inventory questions, promotional campaigns, and post-purchase support across multiple channels. DONNA centralizes communication and standardizes workflows to improve response time, increase conversion rates, and reduce operational friction.

commonWorkflows:
- Product inquiry response
- Order status updates and tracking coordination
- Return and exchange request intake
- Promotional campaign follow-up
- Loyalty program communication
- Inventory availability inquiries

complianceNotes:
Retail operators should ensure:
- Secure handling of customer personal and payment information
- PCI-aligned processes for payment communication
- Clear consent tracking for SMS and promotional outreach
- Documented return and refund communication history

AI systems should:
- Log all customer interactions
- Escalate payment disputes to appropriate staff
- Restrict access to sensitive transaction data
- Maintain clear records for refund or chargeback disputes

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate ecommerce platform and communication channels.
- Deploy automated order confirmation and tracking updates.
- Configure structured return and exchange intake workflows.

60 Day:
- Launch promotional follow-up sequences.
- Implement unified cross-channel communication logging.
- Standardize escalation rules for payment disputes.

90 Day:
- Analyze response time and order resolution metrics.
- Optimize promotional messaging cadence.
- Refine customer segmentation for targeted follow-up.

KPIsToTrack:
- Average response time to customer inquiries
- Order resolution time
- Return processing time
- Conversion rate from inquiry to purchase
- Customer retention rate
