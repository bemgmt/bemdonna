title: Nonprofit Operations with AI

summary:
Nonprofits operate with limited staff, tight budgets, and high communication demands. Donor outreach, volunteer coordination, event management, and grant follow-ups require consistent execution. DONNA provides a structured operational layer that centralizes communication, improves follow-through, and increases visibility without expanding administrative overhead.

commonWorkflows:
- Donor inquiry intake and acknowledgment
- Recurring donation follow-up coordination
- Volunteer application and scheduling
- Event registration and reminder workflows
- Grant deadline tracking and internal task coordination
- Post-event impact reporting communication

complianceNotes:
Nonprofits should ensure:
- Secure handling of donor financial information
- Clear consent tracking for email and SMS campaigns
- Accurate documentation of donation communications
- Role-based access to financial and grant data

AI systems should:
- Log donor and volunteer interactions with timestamps
- Restrict access to sensitive donor financial data
- Escalate grant submission or compliance-related actions to leadership
- Maintain audit trails for board or regulatory review

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate email, SMS, and event registration systems.
- Deploy automated acknowledgment for donations and volunteer sign-ups.
- Configure structured donor inquiry routing.
- Define escalation rules for financial or compliance-related matters.

60 Day:
- Launch recurring donor nurturing workflows.
- Automate event reminders and follow-ups.
- Implement centralized logging for volunteer coordination.

90 Day:
- Analyze donor retention metrics.
- Optimize follow-up cadence for campaigns.
- Refine internal reporting workflows for board visibility.

KPIsToTrack:
- Donor response time
- Donor retention rate
- Volunteer onboarding completion rate
- Event attendance percentage
- Campaign conversion rate
