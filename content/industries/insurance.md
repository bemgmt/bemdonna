title: Insurance Operations with AI

summary:
Insurance agencies manage high volumes of inbound inquiries, policy servicing requests, renewals, and claims coordination. Response speed, documentation accuracy, and compliance are critical. DONNA functions as an operational layer that standardizes communication, structures intake, and ensures consistent follow-through across channels without increasing staffing costs.

commonWorkflows:
- New policy inquiry intake and qualification
- Policy comparison request handling
- Renewal reminders and follow-up coordination
- Claims intake and documentation logging
- Certificate of insurance (COI) requests
- Cross-referrals between agents and carriers

complianceNotes:
Insurance businesses must ensure:
- Secure storage of personal and financial data
- Clear documentation of client communications
- Consent management for SMS and marketing outreach
- Escalation controls for policy changes and binding requests

AI systems should:
- Log all communications with timestamps
- Escalate binding, coverage change, and claims disputes to licensed agents
- Restrict access to sensitive policy data
- Maintain full audit trails for regulatory review

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate CRM and email systems.
- Deploy structured intake for policy inquiries.
- Automate acknowledgment and renewal reminder workflows.
- Define escalation triggers for licensed review.

60 Day:
- Implement claims intake routing workflows.
- Launch cross-channel communication logging.
- Standardize follow-up sequences for quote prospects.

90 Day:
- Analyze quote-to-bind conversion rates.
- Optimize lead qualification criteria.
- Implement referral coordination workflows between agency partners.

KPIsToTrack:
- Lead response time
- Quote-to-bind conversion rate
- Renewal retention rate
- Claims intake response time
- Cross-sell and referral rate
