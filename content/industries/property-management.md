title: Property Management Operations with AI

summary:
Property management firms manage tenant communication, maintenance coordination, leasing inquiries, and compliance reporting across multiple properties. DONNA provides a unified operational layer that standardizes tenant interaction, reduces response time, and improves internal visibility without increasing headcount.

commonWorkflows:
- Tenant maintenance intake and routing
- Leasing inquiry response and tour scheduling
- Rent reminder and payment follow-up coordination
- Vendor dispatch and scheduling
- Tenant renewal communication
- Escalation of urgent or emergency issues

complianceNotes:
Property managers must consider:
- Secure handling of tenant personal data
- Fair housing compliance in communication
- Accurate documentation of maintenance requests
- Audit logs for dispute resolution

AI systems should:
- Log maintenance intake details
- Timestamp vendor assignments
- Maintain tenant communication history
- Restrict access to sensitive financial information

Emergency issues must escalate immediately to human staff.

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate communication channels (phone, SMS, email, web).
- Define maintenance urgency categories.
- Configure structured intake for tenant requests.
- Sync vendor scheduling calendars.

60 Day:
- Automate leasing qualification workflows.
- Launch tour booking automation.
- Implement standardized tenant notification templates.

90 Day:
- Analyze maintenance response time trends.
- Optimize vendor routing efficiency.
- Implement renewal and retention communication sequences.

KPIsToTrack:
- Average maintenance response time
- Tenant satisfaction indicators
- Lease conversion rate
- Vacancy duration
- Vendor turnaround time
