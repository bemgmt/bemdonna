title: Legal Operations with AI

summary:
Law firms manage client intake, consultation scheduling, document requests, and case communication across multiple channels. Speed, accuracy, and compliance are critical. DONNA supports legal operations by structuring intake, centralizing communication, and maintaining clear audit trails while preserving attorney oversight.

commonWorkflows:
- Client intake and qualification
- Consultation scheduling and confirmation
- Document request coordination
- Case status update communication
- Billing inquiry routing
- Deadline reminder and task coordination

complianceNotes:
Legal operations require:
- Strict confidentiality controls
- Secure document handling
- Role-based access to client information
- Clear documentation of communication history
- Escalation of legal advice or binding decisions to licensed attorneys

AI systems should:
- Log client communications with timestamps
- Restrict access to privileged information
- Escalate legal advice requests to attorneys
- Maintain encrypted data storage and transmission

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate CRM and scheduling systems.
- Deploy structured intake qualification workflows.
- Automate consultation booking confirmations.
- Define escalation rules for legal advice boundaries.

60 Day:
- Launch document request and reminder workflows.
- Implement unified communication dashboard.
- Train staff on human-in-the-loop intervention.

90 Day:
- Analyze intake-to-consult conversion rates.
- Optimize intake qualification criteria.
- Refine case communication cadence based on client feedback.

KPIsToTrack:
- Client response time
- Consultation booking rate
- Intake-to-retainer conversion rate
- Case communication response time
- Administrative workload reduction percentage
