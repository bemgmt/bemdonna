title: Hospitality Operations with AI

summary:
Hospitality businesses operate in real time. Guest communication, booking coordination, service requests, and reputation management must function without delay. DONNA provides a unified communications and workflow layer that ensures consistent guest interaction across voice, SMS, email, and web chat while maintaining operational oversight and brand standards.

commonWorkflows:
- Reservation inquiry response and qualification
- Automated booking confirmations and pre-arrival messaging
- Late check-in coordination
- In-stay service requests and maintenance routing
- Post-stay follow-up and review requests
- Escalation of guest complaints or service issues

complianceNotes:
Hospitality operators should ensure:
- Secure storage of guest personal information
- PCI compliance when handling payment-related communication
- Clear documentation of complaint resolution
- Role-based access to reservation data

AI systems should:
- Log all guest interactions with timestamps
- Escalate billing or payment disputes to human staff
- Maintain separation between guest-facing and internal notes
- Provide audit visibility for dispute resolution

implementationPlan:
30 Day:
- Integrate booking system and calendars.
- Consolidate voice, SMS, email, and chat into one dashboard.
- Automate reservation confirmations and pre-arrival instructions.
- Configure structured routing for guest requests.

60 Day:
- Launch automated upsell workflows (late checkout, upgrades).
- Implement complaint escalation rules.
- Train staff on human-in-the-loop takeover procedures.

90 Day:
- Analyze guest response time metrics.
- Optimize pre-arrival and post-stay messaging cadence.
- Refine workflow triggers based on occupancy patterns.

KPIsToTrack:
- Average guest response time
- Booking-to-confirmation speed
- Service request resolution time
- Guest satisfaction score trends
- Review request conversion rate
