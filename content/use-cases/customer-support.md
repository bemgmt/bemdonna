title: Customer Support Automation

painPoint:
Customer inquiries are fragmented across phone, email, and chat. Staff manually respond to repetitive questions, escalate inconsistently, and struggle to maintain context across channels. Response times increase during peak volume, affecting satisfaction and retention.

beforeMetrics:
- Average first response time: 2 to 8 hours
- High volume of repetitive inquiries
- Limited visibility into resolution time

afterMetrics:
- First response time under 10 minutes
- Reduced repetitive tickets by 25 to 40 percent
- Centralized logging and resolution tracking

workflowSteps:
1. Aggregate all inbound communication into unified dashboard.
2. Classify intent using structured routing rules.
3. Provide contextual response from knowledge base.
4. Escalate complex issues to human agent with full history.
5. Log resolution outcome and update reporting metrics.

roiMetrics:
- 15 to 35 percent reduction in support workload
- Improved first-contact resolution rate
- Higher customer satisfaction scores
- Lower operational overhead without additional hires

industries:
- Hospitality
- Retail
- Nonprofits
- Healthcare
- Professional Services
