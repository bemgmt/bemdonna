title: Lead Nurturing Workflows

painPoint:
Not all leads convert immediately. Without structured nurturing, prospects go cold. Manual follow-up is inconsistent and difficult to scale.

beforeMetrics:
- Limited follow-up after first contact
- Low re-engagement rates
- Sales reps manually track long-term prospects

afterMetrics:
- Automated multi-touch nurturing sequences
- Increased re-engagement by 15 to 30 percent
- Structured lead scoring visibility

workflowSteps:
1. Segment leads based on qualification and interest level.
2. Assign automated follow-up cadence.
3. Deliver educational or value-based messages.
4. Monitor engagement signals.
5. Escalate high-intent re-engagement to sales team.

roiMetrics:
- 10 to 25 percent increase in long-cycle conversions
- Reduced manual follow-up time
- Improved lead scoring accuracy
- More predictable pipeline growth

industries:
- Insurance
- B2B Services
- Real Estate
- SaaS
- Financial Services
