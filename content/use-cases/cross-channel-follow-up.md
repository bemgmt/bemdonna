title: Cross-Channel Follow-Up Coordination

painPoint:
Prospects and customers engage across multiple channels. Without coordination, follow-up becomes duplicated or inconsistent, and conversations lose continuity.

beforeMetrics:
- Separate tracking for email, SMS, and phone
- Duplicate follow-ups
- Limited visibility into conversation history

afterMetrics:
- Unified conversation tracking
- Seamless cross-channel continuity
- Reduced duplicate outreach by 20 to 40 percent

workflowSteps:
1. Consolidate all communication channels into unified system.
2. Maintain persistent conversation context.
3. Trigger follow-up on preferred channel.
4. Escalate to human agent when necessary.
5. Log full history for reporting and compliance.

roiMetrics:
- Improved response consistency
- Higher engagement rates
- Reduced communication overhead
- Better customer experience across touchpoints

industries:
- Hospitality
- Retail
- Property Management
- Nonprofits
- Professional Services
