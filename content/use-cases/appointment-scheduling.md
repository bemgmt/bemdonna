title: Appointment Scheduling Automation

painPoint:
Manual scheduling creates friction through back-and-forth communication, double bookings, and missed confirmations. High no-show rates reduce revenue and waste staff time.

beforeMetrics:
- No-show rate: 10 to 30 percent
- Manual scheduling time: 5 to 15 minutes per appointment
- Inconsistent reminder processes

afterMetrics:
- No-show reduction by 10 to 25 percent
- Automated booking within minutes
- Standardized reminder cadence

workflowSteps:
1. Sync real-time calendar availability.
2. Collect required intake details before offering time slots.
3. Offer dynamic appointment options.
4. Send automated confirmations via SMS and email.
5. Trigger reminder and reschedule workflows as needed.

roiMetrics:
- 10 to 25 percent increase in completed appointments
- 20 to 40 percent reduction in scheduling admin time
- Improved client experience through faster booking
- Increased revenue from higher attendance rates

industries:
- Healthcare
- Real Estate
- Legal
- Health and Beauty
- Consulting
