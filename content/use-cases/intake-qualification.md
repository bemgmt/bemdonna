title: Intake and Qualification Automation

painPoint:
Businesses often waste time on unqualified inquiries. Staff manually collect basic information repeatedly, delaying high-value opportunities.

beforeMetrics:
- Manual intake calls averaging 10 to 20 minutes
- Inconsistent qualification criteria
- High percentage of unqualified appointments

afterMetrics:
- Structured qualification within first interaction
- Reduced unqualified bookings by 15 to 35 percent
- Faster routing to correct department

workflowSteps:
1. Collect structured intake information through AI conversation.
2. Validate required criteria before booking.
3. Assign priority tier based on responses.
4. Route qualified prospects to appropriate rep.
5. Log data into CRM with full context.

roiMetrics:
- Increased qualified lead ratio
- Reduced wasted appointment time
- Shorter sales cycles
- Higher close rate from improved targeting

industries:
- Legal
- Healthcare
- Real Estate
- Insurance
- Professional Services
