title: Lead Response Automation

painPoint:
Most SMBs lose revenue not because of weak marketing, but because of slow follow-up. Leads arrive through web forms, phone calls, SMS, and email, but response times vary. Prospects move on quickly when they do not receive immediate acknowledgment or structured next steps.

beforeMetrics:
- Average response time: 1 to 4 hours
- Contact rate: 30 to 50 percent
- Inconsistent follow-up tracking

afterMetrics:
- Average response time: under 5 minutes
- Contact rate: 60 to 85 percent
- Automated follow-up sequences with full logging

workflowSteps:
1. Capture inbound lead across web, voice, SMS, or email.
2. Normalize data into structured CRM record.
3. Send instant acknowledgment aligned with brand voice.
4. Ask qualification questions based on industry or service line.
5. Route to assigned rep or book appointment automatically.

roiMetrics:
- 20 to 40 percent increase in conversion rate
- 15 to 30 percent shorter sales cycle
- Reduced manual follow-up time by 30 to 50 percent
- Improved revenue predictability through standardized intake

industries:
- Real Estate
- Property Management
- Insurance
- Legal
- Home Services
