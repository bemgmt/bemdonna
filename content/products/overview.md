heroTitle: One AI. Every Industry.

shortDescription:
DONNA is a digital operations layer that unifies communication, workflow execution, knowledge, and coordination across small and mid-sized businesses. It does not replace your tools. It connects and standardizes them.

keyFeatures:
- Unified communications across voice, SMS, email, and chat
- Persistent cross-channel conversation context
- Structured workflow execution
- Built-in knowledge base integration
- Human-in-the-loop escalation controls
- Role-based governance and audit logging

practicalExamples:
- A real estate team receives a web inquiry, auto-qualifies the lead, books a showing, logs activity in CRM, and notifies the assigned agent without manual intervention.
- A hospitality property confirms a reservation, sends check-in instructions, handles late arrival questions, and escalates a complaint to management in one continuous workflow.
- A healthcare clinic reduces no-shows by automating confirmations and reminders while maintaining secure patient communication records.

implementationRequirements:
- Existing email provider (Gmail, Outlook, or custom SMTP)
- Calendar system integration
- CRM or lead tracking system (optional but recommended)
- Defined workflow rules and escalation boundaries
- Administrative access for role configuration

FAQ:
Q: Is DONNA a chatbot?
A: No. The chatbot is one interface within a broader operational layer that runs communication and workflows across the business.

Q: Does DONNA replace our existing tools?
A: No. DONNA integrates with existing systems and coordinates them.

Q: How long does implementation take?
A: Basic deployment can begin within weeks depending on integration complexity.

Q: Can we control what DONNA is allowed to do?
A: Yes. Role-based permissions and escalation rules define operational boundaries.

Q: Is this suitable for SMBs or enterprise only?
A: DONNA is built for SMBs but engineered with enterprise-grade architecture.
