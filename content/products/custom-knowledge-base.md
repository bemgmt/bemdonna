heroTitle: Custom Knowledge Base

shortDescription:
The DONNA Custom Knowledge Base is a structured intelligence layer that powers communication, routing, and workflow execution. It transforms business documentation into operational memory that improves accuracy, consistency, and scalability.

keyFeatures:
- Centralized document ingestion (PDFs, SOPs, policies, web content)
- Role-based knowledge segmentation
- Internal and external knowledge separation
- Version control and content ownership tracking
- Context-aware retrieval during conversations
- Continuous refinement through usage signals

practicalExamples:
- A legal firm uploads intake procedures and fee structures. DONNA references the correct language during client intake while escalating legal advice to attorneys.
- A hospitality operator updates cancellation policies. All guest responses immediately reflect the latest approved policy.
- A healthcare clinic standardizes intake instructions. DONNA consistently provides compliant pre-visit guidance across channels.

implementationRequirements:
- Structured documentation (SOPs, FAQs, policy documents)
- Defined content ownership and update cadence
- Role-based access configuration
- Clear separation of sensitive internal information
- Administrative oversight for updates and revisions

FAQ:
Q: Is the knowledge base just a FAQ system?
A: No. It is a structured operational repository that supports internal and external workflows.

Q: Can we restrict access to certain documents?
A: Yes. Role-based permissions allow segmentation of internal and sensitive information.

Q: How often should content be updated?
A: High-change processes should be reviewed monthly. Policies should be audited quarterly.

Q: Does DONNA automatically learn from conversations?
A: Usage patterns can inform refinement, but human oversight ensures accuracy and compliance.

Q: Can multiple departments contribute?
A: Yes. Ownership and update permissions can be assigned by role.
