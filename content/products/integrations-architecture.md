heroTitle: Integrations and Architecture

shortDescription:
DONNA is built as a modular, API-first operational layer designed to integrate with existing business systems. It does not replace your stack. It coordinates it.

keyFeatures:
- API-first architecture
- Multi-LLM support with no single-model dependency
- Email provider integrations (Gmail, Outlook, custom SMTP)
- Telephony and SMS provider integrations
- CRM and business system connectivity
- Modular feature enablement

practicalExamples:
- A real estate brokerage connects its CRM, email, and VOIP provider. DONNA coordinates communication without replacing existing tools.
- A retail brand integrates ecommerce order data and customer support channels for unified visibility.
- An insurance agency connects policy management systems to intake workflows for structured routing.

implementationRequirements:
- API access to existing systems
- Administrative credentials for integration setup
- Defined data flow mapping
- Secure token management practices
- IT oversight for enterprise environments

FAQ:
Q: Does DONNA require replacing our CRM?
A: No. DONNA integrates with existing systems via API.

Q: Are we locked into one AI model?
A: No. The architecture supports multiple models to reduce dependency risk.

Q: How long does integration take?
A: Simple integrations can be configured in weeks. Complex environments may require staged rollout.

Q: Can integrations be disabled?
A: Yes. Modules can be enabled or disabled as needed.

Q: Is custom integration supported?
A: Yes. API-first architecture allows custom connectors where required.
